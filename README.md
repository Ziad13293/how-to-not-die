
Crisis Survival Game

A 3d browser-based survival game built using JavaScript, CSS, and HTML. The player is dropped into three different tactical simulation campaigns where they must survive a couple of animal attacks, with their numbers escalating each level, track supply item drops, and manage resources over 10 increasingly difficult attacks.

Features:
- Three Simulation Biomes: Battle across different biomes with unique lighting filters and custom decorative layouts for each biome.
- Enemy Animal: the enemy tracks your coordinate matrices in real time, rotating toward the player stance adn scaling up in raw speed as levels increase.
- Tinkercad Models: Customized low-poly model assets loaded dynamicallyvia MTLLoader and OBJLoader frameworks to avoid genric lame cube models.
- On-Screen Metrics: Tracking level configurations, ammunition counts, and overall entity threat health directly over the canvas environment.

  Controls table
  WASD Controls for left and right and back and forth
  SpaceBar : shooting ammo
